Getting Started

To start your first consultation:

  1. Click on "New Consultation" from the home screen
  2. Enter the patient's basic information
  3. Click "Start Session" to begin recording
  4. Speak naturally - the AI will transcribe your voice

Currently supported languages include:

  • English
  • Yoruba
  • Hausa
  • Igbo

More languages are being added regularly.

Voice Commands

Command Action
"Delete last line" Removes the last transcribed line
"Insert image" Opens media library for image insertion
"Save note" Saves current consultation
